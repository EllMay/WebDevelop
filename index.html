Warhammer :Age of Sigmar






Grand Alliances | Assembly & Painting | Battles







Age of Sigmar is a tabletop miniature wargame that simulates battle between armies, which consist of Orcs, Elves, Ogres, Humans and Goblins. Two players will face off against armies of 20 to 200+.
You can play the game in different ways, some play very competitively, some have a storyline behind the figures and some just have a go-to get a taste.

There are 3 different ways to play; Open play, Narratvie play and Matched play.
Open play is when not all the rules are followed and the players just have fun with the battle.
Narrative play is whatever story the players want to replicate usually in the style of many role playing games. Matched play is the main way players use.
These matches use a point base system scoring each army usually around 2000 points, each figure is worth a certain amount of points
